Change Spark Seed Phrase

Replace the Spark wallet seed phrase with a different 12-word BIP39 phrase.

This will replace your current Spark wallet seed phrase. If you have not backed up the old seed phrase, you may permanently lose access to that wallet and any funds controlled by it.

Before continuing:

  • - Confirm you have written down the current Spark seed phrase.
  • - Confirm the new phrase is a valid 12-word BIP39 seed phrase.
  • - Spark will restart automatically after the update.

Back Up Your Current Wallet First

This will change the Spark wallet seed phrase and restart Spark automatically. Make sure you have a backup of the current wallet seed phrase before you continue.
